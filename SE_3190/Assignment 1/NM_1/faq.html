Quick Answers About Our Mini Catalog Site

It’s a simple catalog website project for SE/COM S 3190 demonstrating basic multi-page structure, styling, and content.

We showcase six products on each catalog page (Headphones by Mekhi and Gaming Consoles by Ash) for a total of six items across the site.

Team NM_1: Mekhi San and Ash Bhuiyan. Each authored one catalog page and collaborated on the shared design/theme.

We used HTML5, Bootstrap 5, and a tiny custom stylesheet (assets/styles.css) to add theme colors, spacing, shadows, and hover effects.

We are working on two big projects for 3090 and 3190! Keep an eye out for them!
